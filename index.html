About Me

I’m Cassie Sanders — a St. Louis–based web and digital designer who loves turning complex ideas into clean, intuitive, and visually engaging experiences. I’ve spent more than a decade working across web design, UX, content structure, and digital strategy, helping teams bring clarity, usability, and strong visual identity to their online presence.

My work blends creative design with practical front‑end thinking. I enjoy building responsive layouts, refining user flows, and collaborating with writers, marketers, and developers to create digital experiences that feel thoughtful and easy to use. I’m always looking for ways to simplify, improve, and elevate the details that make a site feel polished.

Outside of work, I’m usually exploring new design tools, learning better ways to structure content, or chasing my kids around the backyard. I love what I do, and I bring that curiosity and care into every project I take on.
