Lawson Screen & Digital Products

Website Design

Lawson Screen & Digital Products is a family-owned screen printing machine manufacturing company best known for creating the first automated screen printing squeegee. This company also distributes garment printing supplies as a consumer convenience. To drive higher conversion rates and to target the audience more accurately, two websites coexist for the company; one website for their equipment and the other for supplies.

Webmaster Role

As the webmaster, my tasks included maintaining and updating HTML on Lawson's Joomla platform, managing digital marketing tactics such as online Google ads and email marketing, distributing and creating content on their active blog, Youtube, and social media outlets, and optimizing on-page and off-page search engine optimization.

Project Scope

  • SEO Optimization
  • User Experience Design
  • Professional Website Copywriting
  • Website Content Management System (CMS)
  • Photoshoot Creative Direction
