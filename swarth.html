Sarah Warth

Branding & Web Design

A complete brand identity and digital presence for a still‑life artist known for her meticulous realism. This project spanned logo design, brand development, website design, and creative direction for a custom photoshoot — all crafted to reflect the precision and quiet elegance of Sarah’s work.

The Challenge

Sarah needed a brand that felt refined, intentional, and timeless, matching the precision of her still‑life paintings. Her previous presence didn’t communicate the level of craftsmanship she brings to her work, nor did it support her goals of:

  • Showcasing her portfolio in a clean, elevated way
  • Attracting new collectors and commission clients
  • Presenting herself professionally to galleries
  • Creating a cohesive visual identity across digital and print

The challenge was to build a brand that felt artistic without being decorative, minimal without being cold, and premium without feeling inaccessible.

What I Did

  • Built a complete brand identity including a refined wordmark, color palette, and typography
  • Designed and developed a custom responsive website centered around showcasing artwork
  • Created a gallery system optimized for clarity, scale, and detail
  • Designed a commission inquiry flow to convert visitors into clients
  • Wrote and structured artist bio content to strengthen audience connection
  • Directed the photoshoot to ensure imagery aligned with the brand aesthetic

Results

  • Clearer brand story and stronger visual identity
  • Improved mobile usability and accessibility
  • More intuitive navigation and content structure
  • A flexible design system ready for future growth
