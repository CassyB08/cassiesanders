Sarah Warth

Branding Design

Sarah Warth is a local talented still-life artist who captures details in realism.

Branding Design

Sarah is an asipiring artist who wanted help with getting her name out to her audience. The development of branding her started with a logo. Together with Sarah, we established a color pallete, font choice and a logo. Business cards to hand out to prospects were printed using those elements.

The Process

Web Design

After finalizing the logo and new brand, it was time to build a digital web presence for Sarah to promote her business with. The project goal was to showcase her work and create a direct sales funnel. The website encompasses a robust gallery that emphasizes Sarah's talent, a straightforward contact form for commissions, and insightful information on the artist.
