Vatrix

A modern, responsive web experience for a technology‑driven service provider, focused on clarity, brand cohesion, and a scalable design system.

The Challenge

Vatrix needed a refreshed digital presence that communicated trust, modernity, and technical expertise. Their previous site lacked hierarchy, consistency, and a clear value proposition.

What I Did

  • Refined brand direction and visual identity
  • Designed a modern, responsive homepage and service pages
  • Improved information hierarchy and messaging clarity
  • Built a scalable component system for future expansion
  • Delivered a polished, high‑performance web build

Results

  • Clearer brand story and stronger visual identity
  • Improved mobile usability and accessibility
  • More intuitive navigation and content structure
  • A flexible design system ready for future growth
