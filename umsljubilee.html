University of Missouri Saint Louis

Webpage Design

University of Missouri St. Louis has one of the largest regional public research facilities. UMSL provides high-quality education at an affordable cost. To celebrate the university's 50th anniversary, it held a series of events to commemorate all of the achievements that have taken place in the past five decades.

Webpage Design

UMSL’s graphic design department was challenged to form teams and create an aspiring event to celebrate UMSL’s 50th Anniversary, more commonly known as the UMSL Jubilee. My team of four had won the challenge with the idea of a time capsule event titled: A Dig through the Decades. Alumni and students were encouraged to submit memorabilia to be sealed into a time capsule for 50 years. My team designed flyers and banners to post around campus as advertisements. My involvement included naming the event, devising an online banner to display on the UMSL homepage slider and the landing page with the event’s details and submission form for entries.
