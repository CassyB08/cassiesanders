Unigroup Inc & National Broadband

Page Design

United Van Lines and Mayflower are worldwide moving and relocating service providers. To bring more value to their customer base, they have teamed up with National Broadband to provide internet and television consultations as an after-move service.

Web Page Design

Audiences are channeled from an email source to landing pages built on National Broadband's WordPress platform. These landing pages outline details to help find the best deal and understand what National Broadband can do for them. Working with the National Broadband content writing team, my role pertained to the responsive design of both landing pages. These pages include clear and insightful information on the services offered by National Broadband and lead-driven CTAs and contact forms.

Project Scope

  • Custom, Responsive Website Design
  • User Experience Design
  • Professional Website Copywriting
  • Website Content Management System (CMS)

Building brand trust

Co-branding is imperative to both companies. The user is acknowledged as a customer of either United Van Lines or Mayflower and then given straightforward guidance that National Broadband is the company providing the service of finding internet and television deals for them. To achieve co-branding, logos are side-by-side when delivering a message to the audience. Color scheme and font usage are also appropriately implemented.
