Concordia Technology Solutions

Branding & Web Design

A comprehensive visual and UX refresh of the Concordia Technology Solutions website, focused on clarity, consistency, and conversion across a multi‑product ecosystem.

The Challenge

Concordia Technology Solutions manages a wide suite of church‑focused software products, each with its own legacy visuals, messaging, and page structure. The website needed a modernized, cohesive presentation that:

  • Improved clarity across product pages
  • Strengthened brand consistency
  • Supported SEO and content strategy
  • Increased engagement and conversions
  • Reflected updated product positioning and visuals

This required a system‑level refresh rather than a single‑page redesign.

What I Did

  • Redesigned key product pages for clarity, hierarchy, and conversion
  • Updated navigation and page layouts to improve UX
  • Applied HTML and CSS refinements across the site
  • Collaborated with a writer to restructure content for SEO and messaging clarity
  • Created and integrated new product logos
  • Captured and produced new product screenshots
  • Sourced and implemented updated imagery to modernize the brand
  • Ensured consistency across the entire HubSpot environment

Results

  • A cohesive, modernized web presence across all product offerings
  • Stronger SEO foundation through improved content structure
  • Clearer product differentiation and messaging
  • More intuitive navigation and user flow
  • Increased engagement and improved conversion pathways
  • A scalable visual system that supports future updates
