Lawson Screen & Digital Products

A complete ecommerce and UX modernization for a national screen‑printing supplier, focused on clarity, product discoverability, and conversion performance.

The Challenge

Lawson’s website served thousands of customers but struggled with outdated layouts, inconsistent product pages, and a complex navigation system. The goal was to modernize the experience without disrupting long‑standing customer habits.

What I Did

  • Redesigned product pages for clarity and conversion
  • Improved navigation and information hierarchy
  • Modernized category layouts and product groupings
  • Enhanced support and documentation UX
  • Created a consistent visual system across high‑volume pages

Results

  • +22% increase in page sessions
  • +23% increase in conversion rate
  • Improved product discoverability and user flow
  • More cohesive brand experience across all digital touchpoints
